\[ V' = \frac{4}{3}\pi (2r)^3 = \frac{4}{3}\pi 8r^3 = 8 \left( \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3 \right) = 8V \]
Therefore, if the radius of a sphere is doubled, the volume increases by a factor of 8.
